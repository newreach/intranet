Strategic Directives 1

Housing & Landlord Fair - Washington Park Bridgeport

On Monday August 8th, 2022 New Reach staff along with our partners in Fairfield County, sponsored and participated in a community wide event to engage Landlords and recognize two leaders within the CT housing sector the State Commissioner of Housing Seila Mosquera Bruno and Pack City Communities Executive Director Jilian Baldwin. Through the event providers were able to increase collaborations and raise community impact to support the needs and resources for our clients. The event was also a wonderful opportunity for our staff and other community providers to come together in person to celebrate the work and partnerships with one another. A special shout out to our partners at Alpha Community Services who coordinated this event with all our partners and New Reach staff Machelle Murry who took Lead from New Reach to support this event.



2022 National Alliance to End Homelessness Conference

The week of July 24th, 2022, New Reach staff along with other providers, leaders, advocates and people with lived experience were able to come together in Washington DC for the first National Alliance to End Homelessness Conference, since the start of the Pandemic. During this time, New Reach staff were able to share with other providers the best practices and work New Reach continues to lead in CT, as well as further learn about other National best practices, network and further build upon our relationships and work with our Community Partners.

QA Updates


Deeper Dive into the Data: We have seen an increase in family homelessness since COVID started. The Department of Housing sends out weekly reports with data on how many individuals and families are either in a homeless shelter or living on the streets. When the pandemic first hit, we started trending these data to see what the impact of COVID would be on those experiencing homelessness. While there have been increases in homelessness across the board, there has been a sharper increase in family homelessness. From March 2020 to August 2022, there has been a 62% increase in family homelessness, as compared to a 36% increase in individual homelessness. Here is a link to the dashboard where we trend these data: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/newreach/viz/BNLReport/BNLSnapshot
